Prototype for AI friend-App

Project Description

This project was an assignment to take an existing application and redesign its core purpose. I developed the concept for "Al-buddy and language teacher", a modified version of "Replika", made to support newly arrived individuals in Sweden with their integration.

The application's core purpose is to provide daily mobile support:

  • Language: To support individualized Swedish language learning.
  • Emotional Support: To offer emotional support and increase the understanding of Swedish culture and everyday situations.

I designed a concept, sketches and user interfaces. With Figma a created an interactive prototype with wireframes that displays the functionallity of the app. The design is grounded in principles of Calm Technology and Social Computing.
