Logo Design

Project Description:

MissCurly is a fictional brand created to offer hair products specifically for curly hair. The goal was to build a visual identity that feels playful and accessible rather than exclusive or luxury.

Key design choices for the project:

  • Target Audience: Primarily teenagers and young adults who value a friendly and welcoming brand.
  • Typography: I used Myriad Pro Regular, mixing uppercase and lowercase letters with rounded edges to create a relaxed feel.
  • Color Palette: Different shades of pink were chosen to create a direct connection to the brand name and a feminine aesthetic.
  • Tools: The project was developed using Adobe Illustrator for precision, while Figma and Canva were used for prototyping and social media assets.

The main focus was finding a balance where the logo feels both professional and economically friendly, ensuring customers immediately understand the brand's purpose.

Technologies Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Logotype
  • Mockup
